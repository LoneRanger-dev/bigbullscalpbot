Cancellation & Refunds Policy

Cancellation Policy

Since BigBullScalpBot provides instant access to digital services:

  • Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time

Refund Policy

We have a fair and transparent refund policy:

  • Full refund available within 24 hours of purchase if no signals were accessed

How to Request Cancellation/Refund

To request a cancellation or refund:

  • Contact our support team through Telegram: @bigbullscalpbot_support
  • Email us at: support@bigbullscalpbot.com
  • Include your subscription details and reason for cancellation/refund

Processing Time

Refunds, when approved, will be processed within 5-7 business days depending on your payment method.
